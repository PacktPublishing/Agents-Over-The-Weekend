La Nostra Storia

A journey through tradition, passion, and the finest Italian flavors

La Piada

Born in the heart of Romagna, the piadina is more than just a flatbread — it's a centuries-old tradition. Our recipe has been passed down through four generations, using only the finest flour, extra virgin olive oil, and a pinch of love.

Il Nostro Chiosco

It all started with a small kiosk by the Adriatic coast where Nonna Maria served her famous piadine to fishermen returning from the sea. Today, that spirit of warmth and hospitality lives on in every bite we serve.

Ingredienti Freschi

We source our ingredients from trusted Italian farmers and artisans. From aged Parmigiano Reggiano to hand-picked sun-dried tomatoes, every ingredient tells a story of quality and authenticity.
